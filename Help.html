Fan Club Related Questions

How Do I Log In?

Simple! In fact, you don't even have to make an extra account. You can just sign in through other social media accounts such as Facebook, Google, and Instagram. One you're signed in, you're all set! You can shop for things, comment on Fan Club posts, and maybe even create your very own post about JR II. So, don't hesitate to join in. We welcome everyone!

What Can I Post?

You can post anything, but it must be related to JR II. Posts or comments that are irrevelant or inappropriate will be taken down by mods. Also, we encourage clean language as children and teenagers may also be browsing.

Will JR II Respond to Questions or Posts?

Yes! JR absolutely loves responding to any questions you guys may have. He also loves to read over the posts! If anything, he encourages for fans to ask more questions and learn more about him. Feel free to ask whatever you want.


Music Related Questions

How Often Does JR Release New Music?

We get this question a lot, and unfortunately, the only thing we can tell you is: we don't know. It truthfully depends on many factors that are out of our control, such as JR's musical inspiration, his health, family emergencies, etc. But, as long as you are part of the email list (bottom of homepage), you will be notified whenever something new happens. Stay tuned!

What Music Will JR II Play on Tours?

The website where you can purchase tickets to his tours specifies what music will be played.

Where Can I Buy His Music?

Either on the music tab or you can go to the official JR II store and look in the music tab. If you go to the official store, there are also bundles including music as well as apparel. Go check it out!
