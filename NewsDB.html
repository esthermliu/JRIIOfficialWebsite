March 5, 2019

Free JR II Merchandise Giveaway for the month of April

For the month of April, JR II is giving away free merchandise for his much loved fans. The only thing you need to do is sign up for the JR II official Fan Club and post a picture of yourself. Then, you'll have to follow JR II on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter, and post a comment as well. The free JR II merchandise includes mugs, keychains, necklaces, apparel (sweaters, hoodies, t-shirts, hats), and custom JR II bundles!

February 20, 2019

JR II CELEBRATED HIS BIRTHDAY WITH SOME OF HIS BIGGEST FANS: CHILDREN!

February 20 was JR II's birthday. He decided to visit the local children's hospital and spent the day signing autographs, playing music (school appropriate of course), and making the children laugh! He also gave several lucky children free JR II merchandise! Both the children and JR II had a blast, and by the end of the day, they were all tuckered out and ready to go to bed.
